Child Safety Standards

Last updated: June 14, 2026

CU-Dater ("the App") is developed and published by SPCoolApps. We are fully committed to child safety and strictly prohibit the use of our App by minors.

Age Restriction

CU-Dater is a dating and social-tracking app intended exclusively for users who are 18 years of age or older. By downloading or using the App, you confirm that you are at least 18 years old. We do not knowingly permit anyone under the age of 18 to create an account or use any feature of the App.

No Child-Directed Content

The App does not contain any content directed at children, nor does it collect any personal information from children under 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your country). The App is not designed or intended to attract children.

Prohibition of Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM)

SPCoolApps has a zero-tolerance policy toward child sexual abuse material (CSAM) or any content that sexually exploits minors. Any such content is strictly forbidden and will be reported immediately to the appropriate authorities, including the National Center for Missing and Exploited Children (NCMEC), in accordance with applicable law.

Reporting

If you encounter any content or behavior within the App that you believe involves the exploitation or endangerment of a minor, please contact us immediately at the address below. We will investigate and take prompt action.
